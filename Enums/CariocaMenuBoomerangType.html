CariocaMenuBoomerangType

@objc public enum CariocaMenuBoomerangType : Int

The boomerang type of the menu. - None: Default value. The indicators will always return where they were let. - Vertical: The indicators will always come back at the same Y value. They may switch from Edge if the user wants. - VerticalAndHorizontal: The indicators will always come back at the exact same place
